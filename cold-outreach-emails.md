## 🎯 PRO TIPS FOR SENDING:

### Personalization Keys:
- Research their recent projects on social media
- Check Companies House for their turnover
- Reference local competitions/projects
- Mention mutual connections
- Reference their Google reviews

### Best Send Times:
- Tuesday-Thursday
- 6:30-7:30am (before site)
- 12:30-1:30pm (lunch break)
- 5:30-6:30pm (after site)

### Subject Line Variants That Work:
- "Quick Q about [COMPANY NAME]'s cash flow"
- "[COMPETITOR] just got £45K funded"
- "Re: Your [SPECIFIC PROJECT] funding"
- "Cash today for [TRADE]s in [CITY]"
- "5-min call about instant funding?"

### Follow-Up Strategy:
- Email 1: Day 1
- Email 2: Day 3-4
- Email 3: Day 7-8
- LinkedIn message: Day 10
- Text/WhatsApp: Day 12
- Final email: Day 21

### Response Handlers:

**"How much does it cost?"**
→ "Rates from 1.2% monthly, but let's check what you qualify for first. 5 min call?"

**"Is this secured?"**
→ "Completely unsecured up to £250K. No property, no personal assets at risk."

**"How fast really?"**
→ "Applied Monday 2pm, funded Tuesday 3pm - that fast. Most within 24 hours."

**"We use our bank"**
→ "Perfect for long-term. We're for urgent opportunities banks are too slow for. Keep us as backup?"
