A RIDGID 535 pipe threading machine (£3,500-£4,500) costs from £250/month over 2 years. The larger 1224 model (£8,000-£12,000) is from £580/month over 2 years. Complete commercial plumbing kits (£15,000-£25,000) are from £900-£1,500/month depending on specification.

Absolutely. We finance all RIDGID drain and inspection equipment including K-60 drain machines (£1,500-£2,000, from £110/month), SeeSnake inspection cameras (£3,000-£8,000, from £220-£580/month), NaviTrack locators, and complete drainage kits. Essential for commercial and drainage work.

Yes, we finance all RIDGID press tools including RP 340 press tool (£2,500-£3,500, from £180/month), RP 210 compact press (£1,800-£2,500, from £130/month), and complete ProPress jaw sets. Press tools save huge time vs traditional soldering - finance lets you access this technology immediately.

No. We work with sole traders, partnerships, LLPs and limited companies. Whether you're a self-employed plumber, drainage contractor or commercial plumbing business, if you've been trading 6+ months and have regular income, you can apply for RIDGID finance.

Can I add multiple RIDGID tools to one finance agreement?

Yes. Many plumbers finance complete RIDGID workshops in one agreement - pipe threader + drain machine + inspection camera + press tool. This gives you one monthly payment and better rates than financing items separately. We can include complete tool kits, accessories, and consumables.
