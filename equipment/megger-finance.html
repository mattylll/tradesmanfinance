What are the monthly payments for a Megger multifunction tester?

A Megger MFT1741 multifunction tester (£600-£800) costs from £45/month over 2 years. The MFT1835 (£1,200-£1,500) is from £90/month. Complete electrician test kits (£2,000-£4,000) are from £145-£290/month over 2 years.

Do you finance Megger PAT testers and portable appliance testing equipment?

Absolutely. We finance all Megger PAT testing equipment including PAT150, PAT120, and PAT450 testers (£400-£1,200, from £30-£90/month). Essential for electricians doing commercial PAT testing contracts.

Is Megger as good as Fluke for electrical testing?

Yes. Megger is a premium brand alongside Fluke - both are industry standards. Many electricians prefer Megger for UK testing (18th Edition compliance). Megger often offers better value than Fluke with same professional quality. Both brands are fully financed.

Do I need to be a limited company to finance Megger equipment?

No. We work with sole traders, partnerships, LLPs and limited companies. Whether you're a self-employed electrician or electrical contracting business, if you've been trading 6+ months and have regular income, you can apply for Megger finance.

Can I add multiple Megger testers to one finance agreement?

Yes. Many electricians finance complete Megger test kits in one agreement - multifunction tester + insulation tester + PAT tester + earth loop tester. This gives you one monthly payment and better rates than financing items separately. We can include calibration and cases.
