FLUKE FINANCE FAQs

Can I finance a Fluke 1653B multifunction tester?

Yes. The Fluke 1653B (typically £700-£900) can be financed from £50-£70 per month over 12-24 months. We also finance the Fluke 1664FC, 1662, and all Fluke multifunction testers used by electricians.

What Fluke equipment can I finance?

We finance all Fluke electrical test equipment including multifunction testers (1653B, 1664FC, 1662), clamp meters (376FC, 381), thermal imaging cameras (TiS20, TiS75), PAT testers, insulation testers, and earth ground testers. Finance available from £1,000 to £30,000.

How much does it cost to finance a Fluke 1664FC?

A Fluke 1664FC multifunction tester (typically £1,400-£1,600) costs approximately £100-£120 per month over 12-18 months at representative APR. Final monthly payment depends on your deposit, term length, and credit profile.

Can I finance second-hand Fluke test equipment?

Yes, we finance quality used Fluke equipment from authorized dealers and calibration centres. The equipment must have recent calibration certification and come with at least 6 months warranty.

Do I need to be NICEIC approved to finance Fluke equipment?

No. We work with NICEIC, NAPIT, ELECSA registered electricians and those working towards registration. Self-employed electricians and electrical contractors can all apply.

How quickly can I get Fluke equipment on finance?

Most applications under £5,000 are approved within 4 hours. Once approved, we pay the supplier directly and you can collect your Fluke equipment same day or next day delivery.
