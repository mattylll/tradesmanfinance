FESTOOL FINANCE FAQ

Common questions from carpenters, joiners and cabinet makers

Can I finance Festool track saws and complete carpentry kits?

Yes. We finance all Festool power tools including track saws (TS 55, TS 75, TSC 55), sanders (RO 150, ETS 150, Rotex), routers (OF 1400, MFK 700), dust extractors (CTL MINI, CTL MIDI, CTL SYS), complete Systainer kits, and workshop packages. Flexible 1-5 year terms available.

What are the monthly payments for a Festool track saw package?

A Festool TS 55 track saw package (£600-£800) costs from £45/month over 2 years. The premium TS 75 package (£800-£1,000) is from £60/month. Complete carpentry kits (£3,000-£8,000) are from £200-£500/month depending on configuration.

Do you finance Festool dust extractors and workshop equipment?

Absolutely. We finance all Festool dust extraction systems including CTL MINI (£400-£500, from £30/month), CTL MIDI (£600-£800, from £45/month), CTL SYS (£350-£450, from £25/month), and large workshop extractors. Essential for COSHH compliance and clean professional work.

Can I finance second-hand or refurbished Festool tools?

Yes, we finance both new and used Festool equipment. Second-hand Festool tools hold excellent value and reliability. Many carpenters finance refurbished Festool kits to access premium German engineering at lower cost. Equipment must be in good working condition for finance approval.

Do I need to be a limited company to finance Festool tools?

No. We work with sole traders, partnerships, LLPs and limited companies. Whether you're a self-employed carpenter, joinery business or cabinet making workshop, if you've been trading 6+ months and have regular income, you can apply for Festool finance.

Can I add multiple Festool tools to one finance agreement?

Yes. Many carpenters finance complete Festool workshop setups in one agreement - track saw + sander + router + dust extractor + Systainers. This gives you one monthly payment and better rates than financing items separately. We can include accessories, rails, and consumables too.
