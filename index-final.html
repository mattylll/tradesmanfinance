FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about getting funded

What are the eligibility requirements? +

You need to be a UK registered business, trading for at least 6 months, with a minimum monthly turnover of £5,000. We consider all credit histories and focus on your business performance.

How quickly can I get the money? +

Most applications are approved within 24 hours, and funds are transferred the same day you accept the offer. For larger amounts, it may take up to 48 hours.

What documents do I need? +

Just 3 months of business bank statements and proof of ID. That's it! No lengthy forms, business plans, or accounts required.

Can I pay back early? +

Yes! There are no early repayment penalties. If business is good and you want to clear the loan early, you can do so at any time and save on interest.

Will this affect my credit score? +

Checking your rate is a soft credit check that won't affect your score. We only run a full credit check if you decide to proceed with the funding.
