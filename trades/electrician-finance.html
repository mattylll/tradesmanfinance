ELECTRICIAN FINANCE FAQs

Do I need to be NICEIC approved for electrician finance?

No. We work with NICEIC Approved Contractors, Domestic Installers, NAPIT members, and electricians working towards scheme membership. As long as you're a qualified electrician with Part P, we can help.

Can I finance second-hand electrical test equipment?

Yes, we finance quality used test equipment from reputable dealers. The equipment must be calibrated and come with at least 6 months warranty. Perfect for getting professional kit at lower cost.

Can I include NICEIC/NAPIT membership fees in the finance?

Yes. Annual scheme membership, assessment fees, notification costs, and insurance can all be included in your finance package. Many electricians use this to manage cashflow.

How quickly can I get electrician finance approved?

Most applications get a decision within 4 working hours. For amounts under ¬£10,000, it's often within an hour. Complex cases or amounts over ¬£50,000 may take up to 24 hours.

Can I add more equipment to my existing finance?

Yes. After 3 monthly payments, you can apply for additional finance. Many electricians start with essential kit then add specialist equipment like thermal cameras later.

Do you finance CIS subcontractor electricians?

Yes. Whether you're CIS registered, self-employed sole trader, or limited company electrical contractor, we have suitable finance options. We understand how electrical subcontracting works.
