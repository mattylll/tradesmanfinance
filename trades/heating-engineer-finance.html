HEATING ENGINEER FINANCE FAQs

Do I need to be Gas Safe registered for finance?

Yes for gas equipment finance. We work with Gas Safe registered engineers, those working towards registration, and oil/LPG specialists. As long as you're qualified and trading, we can help.

Can I finance multiple boilers for van stock?

Absolutely. Many engineers finance 5-10 boilers at a time to secure bulk discounts and ensure they never lose a job due to lack of stock. Revolving credit available.

How does seasonal payment work for heating engineers?

We can structure higher payments during busy winter months and lower payments in quiet summer periods. Perfect for matching your cashflow to heating season demand.

Can I include Gas Safe fees in the finance?

Yes. Annual Gas Safe registration, ACS assessments, unvented tickets, and commercial gas qualifications can all be included. Spread the cost of staying compliant.

What about warranty work cashflow?

We understand warranty claims can delay payments. Our working capital loans help bridge the gap while you wait for manufacturer reimbursements.

Do you finance commercial heating projects?

Yes. From school boiler rooms to office installations, we provide project finance up to £150k. Perfect for when materials are needed upfront but payment is 60-90 days.
