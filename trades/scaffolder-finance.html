SCAFFOLDER FINANCE FAQs

Can I finance a Layher scaffold system?

Yes. Layher Allround and Speedyscaf systems (£50,000-£200,000) can be financed over 3-5 years. Typical monthly payments are £1,200-£4,500 depending on system size and deposit. We also finance Haki, Kwikstage, Cuplock and tube & fitting systems.

Do you finance scaffold lorries and hiab trucks?

Yes. We finance 7.5 tonne hiab lorries (£40,000-£70,000), 18 tonne flatbeds (£60,000-£100,000), and curtain-sided rigids. New, used, and ex-fleet vehicles all considered. Finance includes crane mounting if required.

Can I get working capital to fund a large scaffolding contract?

Yes. We provide working capital loans from £10,000 to £500,000 to fund materials, labour, plant hire and sub-contractors. Ideal for bridging the gap between contract start and customer payment (typically 30-90 days).

What is invoice finance for scaffolding contractors?

Invoice finance releases 80-90% of your commercial invoice value within 24 hours, instead of waiting 30-90 days for payment. You stay in control of customer relationships. Perfect for main contractor work with long payment terms.

Can I finance CISRS training and scaffold tickets?

Yes. CISRS Part 1 & 2, Advanced Scaffolder tickets, Trainee Scaffolder courses, Scaffold Inspection (CISRS SI), and supervisor tickets can all be financed. Spread training costs over 12-24 months to manage cash flow.

Do you need to be CISRS registered to get scaffolding finance?

No. We work with CISRS registered scaffolders, those working towards registration, and established scaffolding companies. Whether you're sole trader, limited company or partnership, we can help.

Can CIS scaffolding subcontractors get finance?

Yes. CIS subcontractors are welcome. We understand the scaffolding CIS payment structure and can work around gross or net payment schemes. Many scaffolders operate under CIS for main contractors.

How quickly can scaffolding finance be approved?

Most scaffold system and equipment finance under £50,000 is approved within 4-8 hours. Larger amounts (£100,000+) or complex contracts may take 24-48 hours. Invoice finance can be set up and operational within 5 working days.

Can I finance second-hand scaffold equipment?

Yes, we finance quality used scaffold systems from reputable suppliers. Equipment must be load-tested, have valid certification, and be suitable for continued commercial use. Ex-hire fleet equipment from major suppliers is ideal.

Do you finance scaffold yards and storage compounds?

Yes. We finance secure yard rental deposits, compound purchase, racking systems, forklift trucks for yards, and security equipment. Essential for storing tube & fitting stock and scaffold boards safely.
