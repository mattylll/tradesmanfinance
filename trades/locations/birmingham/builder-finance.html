BIRMINGHAM BUILDER FINANCE FAQs

Can I finance equipment for HS2 work?

Absolutely. HS2 needs thousands of contractors with proper equipment. We finance everything from excavators to environmental monitoring kit. Many builders are scaling up specifically for HS2 opportunities - we help make it happen.

How much can Clean Air Zone compliance save?

Non-compliant HGVs pay £50/day, vans £8/day. Working 250 days annually, that's £12,500 for trucks, £2,000 for vans. Finance a compliant vehicle and the savings often cover much of your monthly payment.

Do you understand Birmingham's payment cycles?

Yes. Whether it's HS2's complex payment structure, council terms, or developer stage payments, we know Birmingham construction. Our finance bridges these gaps so you can take on bigger projects.

Can finance help with Commonwealth Games legacy work?

Definitely. The athlete village conversion and Perry Barr regeneration create huge opportunities. We finance equipment and working capital for builders involved in this historic transformation.

What about Birmingham's industrial conversions?

Birmingham's industrial heritage creates unique opportunities. We finance specialist equipment for converting warehouses, factories, and canal-side buildings into residential and commercial spaces.

Can new building firms get finance in Birmingham?

If you're qualified with experience, yes. Birmingham's construction boom creates space for new businesses. Lower overheads than London mean you can grow profitably. We've helped hundreds make the leap.
