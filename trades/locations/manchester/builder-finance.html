MANCHESTER BUILDER FINANCE FAQs

Can I finance equipment for heritage building work?

Yes. We understand Manchester's mill conversions and listed building projects need specialist equipment. Finance diamond core drills, resin anchoring systems, lime pointing tools, and heritage-approved access equipment.

Do you understand Manchester's regeneration payment terms?

Absolutely. Major developers like Far East Consortium (Victoria North) and Allied London (St John's) often pay on 60-90 day terms. Our invoice finance bridges this gap, releasing up to 90% within 24 hours.

Can I get finance for HS2 and infrastructure projects?

Yes. If you're approved for HS2, Highways England, or Transport for Greater Manchester work, we can finance the specific heavy plant and safety equipment required. We understand infrastructure contractor requirements.

How quickly can I get equipment for Manchester projects?

Same day decisions for most applications. For Victoria North or other major regeneration projects, we can fast-track approvals. Equipment can be on-site within 48-72 hours of approval.

Do you finance Manchester start-up builders?

Yes, if you have construction experience and contracts lined up. Manchester's building boom creates opportunities for new firms. Personal guarantees may be required, but we look at your skills, not just company age.

Can finance help with Greater Manchester Combined Authority requirements?

Yes. GMCA often requires specific insurances, accreditations, and equipment standards. We can include these costs in your finance package, helping you meet tender requirements for public sector work.
