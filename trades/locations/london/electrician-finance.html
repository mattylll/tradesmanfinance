LONDON ELECTRICIAN FINANCE FAQs

Can I finance costs specific to working in London?

Yes. We can include ULEZ-compliant vehicles, congestion charge autopay systems, secure tool storage, tracking devices, and even annual parking permits in your finance package. We understand London's unique costs.

Do you understand London's commercial payment terms?

Absolutely. We know major London firms pay on 60-90 day terms. Our invoice finance releases up to 90% immediately, so you're not waiting months for payment from Canary Wharf corporations or West End retailers.

Can I get finance for Transport for London approved equipment?

Yes. If you're working on TfL, London Underground, or Network Rail projects, we can finance the specific approved test equipment and safety gear required. We work with contractors on major infrastructure projects.

How quickly can I get finance in London?

Same day decisions for most applications under £25k. For larger amounts or complex projects, usually within 24 hours. Funds can be in your account or paid to suppliers within 24-48 hours of approval.

Do you finance London start-up electricians?

Yes, if you're qualified and have some trading history. London's competitive, but there's huge opportunity. We've helped hundreds of London electricians go from employed to running successful businesses.

Can finance help with London's insurance costs?

Yes. London public liability and professional indemnity insurance can be £3,000-10,000 annually. We can include insurance premiums in your finance package, spreading the cost over 12 months.
