LONDON BUILDER FINANCE FAQs

Can I finance equipment for basement excavations?

Absolutely. London's basement boom needs specialist kit - mini diggers, conveyor belts, underpinning equipment, pumping systems. We regularly finance the full setup for basement specialists working in Kensington, Chelsea, and other prime areas.

How do you handle London's payment delays?

We know London developers and main contractors often pay on 60-90 day terms. Our project finance releases funds immediately, so you can pay suppliers and subcontractors without waiting months for your money.

Can finance cover London's site costs?

Yes. We can include parking bay suspensions, hoarding, scaffolding licenses, crane permits, and highway licenses in your finance package. These London-specific costs can run thousands - we help spread them.

What about ULEZ and congestion charges?

Finance a Euro 6 compliant vehicle and save £15/day on ULEZ (£3,750/year for 250 working days). We can also include congestion charge accounts and tracking systems in your package.

Do you understand London's construction market?

Absolutely. We've funded over 1,100 London builders. We know about Section 106 agreements, Thames Water build-overs, party wall disputes, and conservation area restrictions. We get London construction.

Can start-up builders get finance in London?

If you're qualified with some site experience, yes. London's construction boom creates opportunities for new businesses. We've helped hundreds go from employed to running their own successful building firms in London.
