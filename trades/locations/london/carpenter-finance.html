LONDON CARPENTER FINANCE FAQs

Can I finance a workshop in expensive London?

While we don't finance property, we can help kit out your workshop with space-efficient machinery. Combination machines, wall-mounted dust extraction, mobile bases - everything to maximise productivity in minimal London space.

Do you understand heritage restoration requirements?

Absolutely. We regularly finance tools for listed building work - mortise machines for traditional joints, moulding planes, steam bending equipment. We know conservation officers demand authentic techniques.

Can finance help with luxury project materials?

Yes. London's high-end market often needs ¬£20k+ of materials upfront. Our working capital solutions bridge the gap. We've funded American black walnut orders, Blum hardware packages, and designer surface materials.

What about London's ULEZ and parking costs?

Finance a Euro 6 van to avoid ¬£12.50 daily ULEZ charges. We can include professional racking, parking permit costs, and even congestion charge accounts in your package. Save thousands annually.

Do you finance CNC equipment for small workshops?

Yes. Modern CNC routers are perfect for London workshops - precise, space-efficient, and versatile. We finance entry-level CNCs from ¬£15k to industrial models over ¬£50k, plus training if needed.

Can new carpentry businesses get finance in London?

If you're qualified with some experience, yes. London's premium market rewards quality craftsmanship. We've helped many employed carpenters set up successful independent workshops across the capital.
