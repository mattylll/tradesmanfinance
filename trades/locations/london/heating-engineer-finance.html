LONDON HEATING ENGINEER FINANCE FAQs

Can I finance heat pump training and equipment together?

Absolutely. The MCS certification plus ASHP equipment can be bundled into one package. We regularly finance the complete transition from gas to renewables for London engineers. Typical package: £15-25k.

Do you understand London's commercial heating market?

Yes. We know City offices need 24/7 reliability, hotels can't have downtime, and hospitals have strict compliance. We finance everything from cascade boilers to BMS systems for London's commercial sector.

What about London's green initiatives?

London's leading on net-zero. We finance heat pumps, solar thermal, hydrogen-ready boilers. Many boroughs offer grants - we help you get equipped to access these opportunities.

Can finance help with parts inventory?

Yes. London customers expect same-day fixes. We finance parts stock for common breakdowns - pumps, valves, controls. Having parts on hand means more first-time fixes and happy customers.

Finance a Euro 6 van and save £12.50/day on ULEZ. We can include accounts for congestion charging, plus tracking for automatic payments. These savings often cover half your finance payment.

If you're Gas Safe registered with some experience, yes. London's heating market has room for quality engineers. We've helped hundreds transition from employment to running successful heating businesses.
