BUILDER FINANCE FAQs

Can I finance used construction plant?

Yes, we finance quality used excavators, telehandlers and dumpers up to 10 years old from established dealers. Equipment must have service history and come with warranty.

How does project finance work for builders?

We structure funding to match your project stages. Draw down funds as needed, pay interest only during build, then convert to term loan on completion. Perfect for developments.

Can I include CITB levy and training costs?

Yes. CITB levy, CSCS cards, NVQ assessments, plant operator tickets and H&S training can all be included in your finance package to manage cashflow.

Do you understand CIS tax deductions?

Absolutely. We work with CIS contractors daily and understand how 20-30% tax deductions affect cashflow. Our underwriters factor this into affordability calculations.

What about retention money on contracts?

We can advance up to 90% against certified work including retention. Don't wait 12 months for your 5-10% retention - release it now to fund the next job.

Can I get finance with a new construction company?

Yes, if directors have construction experience. We look at your track record in the trade, not just company age. Personal guarantees may be required for new firms.
