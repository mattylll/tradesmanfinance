PLUMBER FINANCE FAQs

Do I need to be Gas Safe registered for plumber finance?

No. We work with Gas Safe registered plumbers, Water Regs qualified plumbers, and those specialising in commercial pipework or bathrooms. As long as you're a qualified plumber, we can help.

Can I finance second-hand plumbing tools?

Yes, we finance quality used pipe threading machines, press tools and drain cameras from reputable dealers. The equipment must be serviced and come with at least 6 months warranty.

Can I include Gas Safe registration fees in the finance?

Yes. Annual Gas Safe fees, ACS assessments, Water Regs certification, and liability insurance can all be included in your finance package. Many plumbers use this to manage cashflow.

How quickly can I get plumber finance approved?

Most applications get a decision within 4 working hours. For amounts under £15,000, it's often within an hour. Complex cases or amounts over £50,000 may take up to 24 hours.

Can I add more equipment to my existing finance?

Yes. After 3 monthly payments, you can apply for additional finance. Many plumbers start with essential tools then add specialist equipment like drain cameras or power flushing units later.

Do you finance CIS subcontractor plumbers?

Yes. Whether you're CIS registered, self-employed sole trader, or limited company plumbing contractor, we have suitable finance options. We understand how plumbing subcontracting works.
